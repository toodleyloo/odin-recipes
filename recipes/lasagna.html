  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Drain excess fat.
  4. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, salt, and pepper.
  5. Spread a layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  6. Layer 3 lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  7. Add half of the ricotta mixture, half of the ground beef, and a third of the mozzarella cheese.
  8. Repeat the layers: sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, ground beef, and mozzarella cheese.
  9. Top with remaining noodles, marinara sauce, and the rest of the mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  10. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  11. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  12. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving.
  13. Enjoy your delicious lasagna!
